Backdoor Bandits 9

A new way to secure your doors

The IEEE lab at our school has two entrances: the front and back. The front can be opened with a key, but currently people are unable to come in through the backdoor without it already being propped open. I'm working on a method that would allow officers to come in through the back door securely and efficiently. The officers will be given a special IR key that will activate a group of ultrasonic transducers. The user will then use a secret knock that will unlock the door and push it open.
